Vision

You're building an epic fantasy RPG with the tactical depth and player agency of Baldur's Gate 3, but grounded in a slightly cartoonish visual style that feels approachable and timeless. This is a game where strategy, choice, and combat mastery collide in a grand, fantastical world.

Genre and Inspirations

Your foundation is role-playing adventure with turn-based tactical combat. What draws you to Baldur's Gate 3 is its tactical combat system where every move matters, positioning counts, and player decisions shape outcomes. You want that same deliberate, strategic feel: players choosing actions carefully, weighing options, and experiencing real consequences. This isn't action-based button mashing. This is thinking ahead.

Look and Feel

Fantasy with a slightly cartoonish touch. Think detailed worlds and characters that don't aim for photorealism, but instead lean into charm, clarity, and visual personality. The mood is epic and grand - sweeping vistas, dramatic lighting, the sense that adventure is unfolding at scale. The tone feels heroic and immersive without being grim or overly serious.

Core Mechanics

Turn-based strategy combat is your heartbeat. Players move characters, position them on a battlefield, choose attacks and abilities, and watch consequences unfold. Outside combat, exploration and decision-making matter. The game loop is: explore the world, encounter challenges or enemies, engage in thoughtful tactical battles, earn rewards, and continue your journey. Every battle should feel meaningful, not like filler.

What Makes It Special

The blend of grand scope with approachable aesthetics creates something inviting yet weighty. Many fantasy RPGs feel intimidating or overly complex. Yours has the strategic depth players crave, but wrapped in a visual style that feels warm and accessible. That's your differentiator: serious tactics, epic adventure, but a world that welcomes players in rather than gatekeeping them.
